About Us

Who we are

We've created a company that is made up of human relationships, not transactions.

  • We want to help people find their next project or permanent opportunity within a company..Perhaps our network will lead to that. We just want to see people work, it’s that simple.
  • We want to be the company that people refer their friends and family to when they see one of our opportunities that matches their interests or needs. We want to be known as the trusted company that is ‘doing it right.’
  • While we know that you can’t work with just one staffing firm, we want to be one of the first companies you call when you are looking for your next opportunity, your next contract resource or your next employee. We want to be the company you prefer to work with and do new business with.
  • We have an attitude of gratitude. Many people helped us get to this point in our journey, so we’re excited to pay them back by paying it forward.

Referrals

Want a bigger payday?

As you review our new opportunity postings, think of everyone you know that might be interested, if you aren’t. Pass the opportunity on and encourage them to learn more about the opportunity and STAR Collaborative!

If we successfully place the person you refer to us, we will give you:

  • Contract: $1/hour for every hour they bill on that project as long as they are billing on that project
  • Direct Hire: $1,000 after 90 successful days on the opportunity.

Tell your friend or colleague to select ‘Payday Challenge’ from the online application and add your name as the referrer. We’ll take it from there!

The Fine Print: This program is subject to modification or discontinuation at our discretion. State or Federal taxes may apply. Payments will be made every Quarter days for the duration of the project based on hours invoiced/approved.
