Why choose STAR?

We’ve been quietly disrupting the Twin Cities market inspiring interest,creating energy, and fostering respect and loyalty through our work, and:


We won't bring you donuts

We run lean. No gimmicks. No fuss. Using tech, we minimze our overhead to maximize your payout.

Donuts or dollars?

Just so we’re clear up front, if we successfully place you in a project at one of our clients, we’re not going to randomly drop in and bring you donuts. We are not going to give you STAR-themed swag like pens, notebooks or t-shirts. Finally, we do not have a box suite for the Twins, Wild, or Vikings, so don’t look for a game invite from us.

It’s not that we don’t care or want to hang with you, it’s just that we thought you’d rather get more money in your pocket from the bill rate on the project, and isn’t that a lot more exciting than a bunch of junk food or chachkies that will collect dust on your shelf?

We let the rabbit out of the hat

No secrets. No surprises. We let you know the opportunity details, bill rate, and get your permission before submitting for you for any role.

We know you can’t work with just one firm. We just want to be your favorite.

Like a magician who tells you how the trick is performed, STAR Collaborative’s transparent business model has exposed the staffing and consulting industry’s secrets. Here are a few examples of our transparency:

  • When we work with you on a potential contracting role, we tell you the client bill rate, and your pay rate, so you know exactly your share of the bill rate.
  • If we think you’re a good match, we’ll get your permission to present your resume. We never submit a resume without your knowledge and approval.
  • You get access to ALL of the opportunities we receive, which are instantly viewable on our website or delivered to you via email subscription.
  • We’ll let you know if you’re a good fit for the role you are interested in, and if we think presenting you to the client is a good idea.
  • We’ll give you honest and direct feedback, and we’ll help you maximize your resume to ensure marketability.
  • As we work with our clients as they review and vet candidates, we’ll provide you with any feedback provided so we can adapt and improve.

Even though we think these things are common sense, they’re not always common practice in the industry. Our goal is to create a positive candidate experience for you based on no secrets or surprises.” to make the last line it’s own line.

We've been in your shoes

We know who and what make the best project and contract experiences. We work hard to ensure you have both.

How many staffing firms can literally say they’ve been in your shoes?

It’s often said that you can’t understand a person unless you’ve walked a mile in their shoes. If that’s the case, we’ve walked more than a few marathons in understanding the challenges you face in your role!

STAR’s owners, operations team, and recruiters have actually worked on your side of the table. We were the internal corporate team members responsible for sourcing, managing, and maintaining the quality of third party contractors and consultants. We’ve led massive, cross-functional program and project teams and had to assemble the right portfolio of talent to get the job done. We’ve led PMOs and internal vendor management and resource programs. Basically, we’ve been the candidate, client, hiring manager, vendor and resource manager (we are younger than it seems)!

Not only that, we know the operations, logistics, and procurement steps necessary to get talent sourced, screened, onboarded and running in an efficient and effective way. We know the internal demands to finding the right talent, and we do our best to make your job easier.

No longer a start up, STAR has eight years under its belt of serving more than 70 clients and placing hundreds of contract, contract-to-hire, and direct hire candidates at all size companies: small startup to Fortune 50 VMS programs. We can handle any size or volume talent needs, thanks to our unique knowledge of the business of doing business.

About Us

Who we are

We've created a company that is made up of human relationships, not transactions.

  • We want to help people find their next project or permanent opportunity within a company..Perhaps our network will lead to that. We just want to see people work, it’s that simple.
  • We want to be the company that people refer their friends and family to when they see one of our opportunities that matches their interests or needs. We want to be known as the trusted company that is ‘doing it right.’
  • While we know that you can’t work with just one staffing firm, we want to be one of the first companies you call when you are looking for your next opportunity, your next contract resource or your next employee. We want to be the company you prefer to work with and do new business with.
  • We have an attitude of gratitude. Many people helped us get to this point in our journey, so we’re excited to pay them back by paying it forward.

Our Values

  • Connected: We are in the relationship business.
  • Disruptive: We like to challenge ourselves and the industry
  • Abundant: Everyone can win.
  • Transparent: We value honesty and help our clients and candidates to understand the “why”.
  • Fair: We value integrity.
  • Lean: We accomplish a lot with little.
  • Respect: We treat others the way we want to be treated.

Want a bigger payday?

As you review our new opportunity postings, think of everyone you know that might be interested, if you aren’t. Pass the opportunity on and encourage them to learn more about the opportunity and STAR Collaborative!

If we successfully place the person you refer to us, we will give you:

  • Contract: $1/hour for every hour they bill on that project as long as they are billing on that project
  • Direct Hire: $1,000 after 90 successful days on the opportunity.

Tell your friend or colleague to select ‘Payday Challenge’ from the online application and add your name as the referrer. We’ll take it from there!

The Fine Print: This program is subject to modification or discontinuation at our discretion. State or Federal taxes may apply. Payments will be made every Quarter days for the duration of the project based on hours invoiced/approved.
