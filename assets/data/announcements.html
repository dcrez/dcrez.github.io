Rules Updates

A series of updates have been made to the rules. These changes are:

  • Mini Rod: If your tractor is under 370 cubic inches, you can add 50lbs to your tractor.
  • Stock Altered through Mini Rod: must wear a full fire suit, jacket, and pants
    • We understand that this rule may cause hardship for some, but there are many pullers that have extra suits you can borrow.
    • This is for the safety of our pullers, and the professionalism of our club.
  • Fuel: Nitros is not allowed. This has been a known rule, but we changed the verbiage in the rule book in all classes to reflect it so there are no questioned as to what is allowed.
