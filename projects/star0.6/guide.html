Applicant Guide

Whether you are looking for a full time position or a contracting role, we have a wide range of companies that offer interesting and exciting opportunities.

Our application process

We’ve automated the data gathering aspects of the application process so that we can spend more time finding ways to showcase your skills and experience, and to do it in as rapid a fashion as possible. Our clients move quickly, so we need you to help us package your candidacy as accurately and quickly as possible.

Please note that we only submit fully-vetted and qualified candidates to our clients, so consider this the first step to helping us get to know you! We recognize that this process takes extra effort, but we know it pays off in capturing the attention of our clients. It also has a halo effect of generating good content that you can leverage elsewhere – such as your LinkedIn profile.

Apply

You can find all our opportunities online or via email subscription. Click here to subscribe or manage your subscription preferences.

Once you've found an opportunity that interests you, click "apply" (there's a link to apply directly from the opportunity whether you're viewing it in your email or on the web). You'll be directed to a page asking for your name and contact details. We will not submit you for an opportunity without your consent.

Prepare your resume

Reformat your resume using our STAR Resume Template. To increase your chances of getting an interview, tailor page 1 of your STAR resume to the opportunity job posting you are applying for.

Need help with the template? Download the STAR Resume Guide for step-by-step guidance.

Provide additional information

As soon as you apply online, you will receive an email asking for additional details such as phone number, rate requirements, availability and other background questions. It also asks for key selling points to ensure the client knows why you are someone they should consider for the job, over someone else. Our team (and several of our clients) will receive this information real-time via email workflow.

Stay tuned

You will receive email updates from our automated system, or a Candidate Relations Project Manager (CRPM), explaining your status and what to expect.
