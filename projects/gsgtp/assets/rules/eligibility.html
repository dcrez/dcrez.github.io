1. Contest is open to two wheel drive, rear wheel drive tractors.
  2. Drivers under the age of 18 must have a parental/guardian signed consent form on file (this is only necessary to be signed once per season) parent/guardian must be present at the pull.
  3. Stock and Stock Altered classes the minimum age is 6 years old.
    1. Sport Stock, Pro Stock, Sportsman Modified and Super Modified, the minimum age is 12 years old.
    2. Open Classes minimum age is 16.
    3. In all classes driver must maintain control of the tractor at all times.
  4. All tractors must pass a safety inspection by a GSGTP official annually. GSGTP inspection decal must be displayed near the kill switch on rear of the tractor. Refusal of inspection disqualifies eligibility.
  5. It is mandatory that all drivers attend any driver meetings.
  6. No vehicle may exceed 6' in width, nor shall any portion extend more than 8' in front of center of the rear axle. Front tires must track within the tread of the rear tires.
  7. Judgment calls by GSGTP/event officials are final and non-appealable.
  8. Engine may not be used as a primary support structure for the vehicle. Engine may not be used as part of frame.
